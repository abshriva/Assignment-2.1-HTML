Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a Recipe of rich Scrambled eggs

Ingrdients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix theeggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add crea and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the egde of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove rom the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate untill it served)
